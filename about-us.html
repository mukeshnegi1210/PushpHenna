About Pushp Henna 

Growing with Innovation

We introduce ourselves as a renowned manufacturer and exporter of an impeccable range of Natural Mehandi, Mehandi Cone, Herbal Mehandi and Hair Care products. Since our inception years back, we have procured stupendous experience in crafting Henna products both for hands as well as hair. Our complete collection is crafted by making use of natural and pure herbs which are of the finest quality as raw materials. These raw materials have been acquired from quality-oriented suppliers.

We do not mix any harmful chemical substances in our products. Our products have gained high acclaim from our valued customers because of their excellent results and without any side effects.

What Makes Us Different

Being Sojat based products manufacturing entity, Pushp Henna Private Limited has consistently been growing in the market. It has grown at a speed of nautical miles. Having established of herbal shades . The never ending efforts of our experts are the major reasons for our timely growth and successful rise on the domain which we aim to continue in long run. Supported by a network direct distributors and numerous indirect distributors under the support of marketing experts, our company has successfully extended the distribution of most demanded Hair care products. Our products are ready to grab the attention of remote markets of the country. our firm has earned a client base of retail counters serving end users. We aim to expand the reach of our products to 10 additional countries soon.

Our Story Of Success

Pushp Henna Private Limited established in 2001 manufacturer, exporter and supplier of mehandi. Serving the other entities of Hair Care industries, we are nationally acknowledged supplier of mehandi from Sojat. Achieving immense milestones in this field of expertize, we have stepped into manufacturing and processing of hair color and henna etc.
