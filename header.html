    Automated Development Rights Transfer System

    NCDEX e Markets Ltd., (NeML) is India’s leading fully integrated e-markets company has been mandated by the Karnataka Urban Infrastructure Development and Finance Corporation (KUIDFC) to develop a delivery-based e-market place for buyers and sellers of Developmental Rights Certificates (DRCs) in the country. This e-marketplace called Automated Development Rights Transfer System (ADRTS), is the first and only comprehensive live e-market platform for buying and selling of DRCs in the country and is designed and developed by NeML in close collaboration with Karnataka Government.
